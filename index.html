About Us

Elite Team Fresno is a Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu school that provides high-quality classes from some of the valley's greatest Jiu-Jitsu competitors. Our mission is to provide a safe, supportive, and challenging environment where students of all ages and skill levels can grow and achieve their personal goals.

Head Coach Marty Robles has been practicing BJJ for over 10 years and received his black belt from Tom Knox, Elite Team's Program Director and Professor.

Our Coaches

Professor Marty Robles

Head Instructor

Head Coach Marty Robles has been practicing BJJ for over 10 years and received his black belt from Tom Knox, Elite Team's Program Director.

Marty is a multiple-time competition winner and is dedicated to passing on his wealth of knowledge to students of all ages. His passion for the sport is evident in every class he teaches.

Coach Jane Smith

Kids Program Director

Specializes in developing young athletes. Her passion for Jiu Jitsu and positive attitude make learning fun for kids.

Jane is a brown belt and has been coaching for over 5 years. She is known for her patience and ability to break down complex techniques into simple, easy-to-understand steps for children.

Instructor Mike Johnson

Advanced Class Instructor

A fierce competitor and dedicated coach, he brings a modern, competition-focused perspective to our advanced classes.

Mike is a black belt and an active competitor on the tournament scene. He specializes in leg locks and modern BJJ techniques, ensuring our advanced students are always ahead of the curve.
