About Us

Elite Team Fresno is a Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu school that provides high-quality classes from some of the valley's greatest Jiu-Jitsu competitors. Our mission is to provide a safe, supportive, and challenging environment where students of all ages and skill levels can grow and achieve their personal goals.

Head Coach Marty Robles has been practicing BJJ for over 10 years and received his black belt from Tom Knox, Elite Team's Program Director and Professor. Whether you're looking to get in shape, learn self-defense, or compete at the highest levels, we have the expertise to help you succeed.
