Your First Class Experience

Before You Arrive

1
Wear comfortable athletic clothing (shorts and t-shirt)
2
Bring water bottle and towel
3
Remove jewelry and trim fingernails
4
Arrive 15 minutes early for paperwork

Class Structure

1
10-minute dynamic warm-up
2
25-30 minutes technique instruction
3
Partner drilling and repetition
4
Optional light sparring (advanced students)

Remember: Everyone was a beginner once! Our instructors and students create a welcoming environment where you can learn at your own pace. Focus on having fun and learning - everything else will follow naturally.
